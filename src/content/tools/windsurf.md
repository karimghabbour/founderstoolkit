Windsurf (formerly Codeium Editor) is an AI IDE that combines traditional code completion with agentic workflows. Its Cascade feature can plan and execute multi-step coding tasks across your project.

A strong choice for solo founders who want an AI coding assistant at a lower price point. The free tier is generous for getting started.
