IFTTT (If This Then That) lets you create simple automations between apps, devices, and services. It is especially strong for smart home and IoT use cases, connecting devices like Philips Hue, Ring, and Google Home. The free plan supports up to 5 applets with single trigger-action pairs, while Pro ($2.99/mo) and Pro+ ($8.99/mo) unlock multi-step applets, faster execution, and filter code.
